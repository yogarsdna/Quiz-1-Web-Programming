Ayam Betutu


Betutu Chicken is a side dish made from whole chicken or duck stuffed with spices, then roasted in a husk fire. Betutu Chicken is well known in all districts in Bali. Betutu Chicken is a type of traditional Balinese food whose raw materials are whole duck and chicken carcasses.

Tipat Cantok


Tipat Cantok is a typical Balinese food made from boiled vegetables and ketupat and served with peanut sauce. In Balinese, Tipat has the meaning of "ketupat" and Cantok has the meaning of "pulled/mashed with a mortar".

Sate Lilit


Sate Lilit is a variant of satay from Bali. This satay is made from minced pork, fish, chicken, beef, or even turtle, then mixed with grated coconut, coconut milk, lime, red onion, and pepper.
